* Difficult Cases

** BIND in the Constraint

#+BEGIN_SRC SPARQL
?s ?p ?o
BIND(COUNT(?o) AS ?count)
#+END_SRC

when ?o is substituted with a value:

#+BEGIN_SRC SPARQL
?s ?p <property>
BIND(COUNT(<property>) AS ?count)
#+END_SRC SPARQL

One solution(?):

#+BEGIN_SRC SPARQL
?s ?p <property>
BIND(COUNT(<property>) AS ?count)
#+END_SRC SPARQL
